City, University of London students passionate about technology.
Our goal is to provide oppotunities for City students, from all backgrounds, to learn more about technology and share with eachother.

What do we do?

Hackathons

We go to hackathons across the UK and Europe. We have multiple hackathons of our own planned too, so stay tuned!

Workshops

We run student-lead workshops on everything from web development, mobile development and Git.

Tech Talks

We host regular talks by speakers from the local community and the wider tech industry.
